We have something fun for everyone!

Events

Family Olympics held every 1st and 3rd weekend of each month from Spring to fall.Family summer Olympics are a great way to spend time with the family. Kids and adults can put their competitive spirit to the test with a variety of games and competitions. Such as water balloon relay, jumbo beer pong, wheelbarrow, potato sack, and 3 leg races. Families compete for various prizes; top 3 families win Great escape Olympic medals.

Community roast bonfire held every night at 11 pm from spring to fall, bring the marshmallows, chocolate, and graham crackers. BYOB is always an option for guest 21 and older. Fireworks accompany the bonfire on the last Saturday of every summer month.

Movies under the stars every Friday and Saturday night from spring to fall (weather permitting). Enjoy movies under the stars on one of our 2 large projector screens, BYOC/BYOB. Grab the chairs and the blankets and snuggle up under the stars or swim up to a pool seat and enjoy the movie from the comforts of the pool.
