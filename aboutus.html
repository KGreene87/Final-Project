COME FOR THE VIEWS, STAY FOR THE FUN!!

Here at The Great Escape Retreat aim to make a camper out of you. We set out to create a family friendly camping experience that can be fun for all. Here we have River Tubing, Fishing, Canoe rentals, Family Olympics, Hiking Trails, CLubhouse with fitness center,Swim in pool, hot tubs, clean restrooms through out, showers, and laundry room.

Nestled in the hills of northern Georgia we have over 200 camping spaces. There is a modern campground that offers 55 modern styled cabins with large glass doors and windows and sky lights in every unit. There are 60 RV campsites with free RV dumping station, 60 Dome style glamping tents. We also offer backcountry campsites. We have large bonfires nightly for community marshmallow roast. Private area of the park with 20 luxury tents make up our lovers lane.
